Efstratios Gavves

Temporal Leraning & Neural Dynamics

Associate Professor, UvA

Co-founder, Ellogon.AI

What is the role of time in machine learning, vision, and algorithms? In dynamical, non-stationary, non i.i.d., open-ended word this question fascinates me and drives my research, especially in the interface with the sciences, where temporality is omnipresent. Learning algorithms that understand time and dynamics will alse lead to algorithms that are reliable, robust, explainable, algorithms that extrapolate.

With my students, embedded in VISLab, we explore temporal machine learning and dynamics in many fronts: from neural system dynamics and symmetries to computational memory, dynamical systems and causality, with applications from fluid dynamics to biomedical, astronomy, climate science, oncology, and more.

My research is supported by personal European (ERC StG) and Dutch grants (NWO VIDI), as well as academic-industry collaborations in the form of ICAI labs (QUVA with Qualcomm, POP-AART with Elekta.
