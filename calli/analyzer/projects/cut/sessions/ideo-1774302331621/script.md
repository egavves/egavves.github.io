# CUT ANALYTICS — FULL VIDEO SCRIPT v3.0

**"EXES: Truth or Drink — But Their New Partners Are Watching"**

**Format:** Two exes (JORDAN, 24 & MAYA, 23) sit across from each other at a small round table. Their current partners (SAM, 25 & DEV, 24) sit together in a hidden room behind a ONE-WAY MIRROR, watching in real time. Each partner holds a single RED CARD — good for one intervention: they can send in ONE question of their own at any point during the game. The exes know the mirror is there. They don't know *when* the cards will come.

**Duration Target:** 5–7 min edited / ~45 min raw shoot

---

## COLD OPEN — THE SIT-DOWN (0:00–0:20)

[CAMERA starts TIGHT on two empty chairs at a small round table. A bottle of Casamigos tequila sits between two shot glasses. Behind JORDAN's chair, visible but slightly out of focus: a large dark pane of glass — the one-way mirror. Ambient silence. CAMERA racks focus as JORDAN enters frame left, sits. He clocks the mirror. Shifts in his seat. Beat. MAYA enters frame right. They lock eyes. JORDAN exhales sharply through his nose — half-laugh, half-dread.]

JORDAN: "Oh, absolutely not."

MAYA: [sitting down, pulling chair in, glancing at the mirror] "Wow. You still do that thing with your jaw when you're nervous."

JORDAN: "I'm not nervous."

MAYA: "Then why won't you look at the glass?"

[JORDAN's eyes flick to the mirror — just for a second — then back to MAYA. He knows they're behind it. He just doesn't know what they're thinking.]

[SMASH CUT to MIRROR ROOM. SAM and DEV sit side by side on a couch facing the glass. They can see everything. SAM leans forward, arms crossed. Each of them holds a RED CARD in their lap.]

SAM: "Yes you are, babe."

DEV: [turning the red card over in their fingers, quiet] "This is gonna be something."

[TITLE CARD SLAMS IN: **EXES: TRUTH OR DRINK — BUT THEIR NEW PARTNERS ARE WATCHING** — accompanied by a heavy bass hit.]

---

## SCENE 1 — "THE RULES" (0:20–1:00)

[WIDE SHOT of the table. The one-way mirror dominates the background — a dark, reflective wall. A PRODUCER's hand enters frame and places a stack of question cards face-down between them.]

JORDAN: [to camera] "So we dated for…"

MAYA: "A year and a half."

JORDAN: "A year and four months."

MAYA: [deadpan] "And he's already wrong."

[CUT TO MIRROR ROOM. DEV laughs, shaking their head. SAM doesn't laugh.]

DEV: "She's gonna eat him alive."

SAM: [eyes on Jordan through the glass] "He better not fumble this."

[BACK TO TABLE.]

JORDAN: "Okay. Rules. You pull a card, you read it out loud, the other person either answers honestly—"

MAYA: "—or you drink."

JORDAN: [gesturing vaguely toward the mirror] "And our current partners are… behind that."

MAYA: "Allegedly."

[JORDAN stares at the mirror. His own reflection stares back. He can't see through it. He waves — small, awkward. Nothing happens.]

[CUT TO MIRROR ROOM. SAM watches him wave. Doesn't wave back. Just exhales.]

SAM: "Idiot."

[DEV snorts.]

[BACK TO TABLE.]

JORDAN: "Cool. Cool cool cool. This is fine."

MAYA: [pouring two shots] "Oh — and apparently they each get to send in one question. Whenever they want."

JORDAN: [beat — this is new information landing on his face in real time] "They what?"

MAYA: "One question each. Their choice. Could come any round."

[JORDAN looks at the mirror again. Different energy now. The glass isn't just watching — it's *armed*.]

JORDAN: "…Cool."

MAYA: [sliding a shot toward him] "Let's go."

---

## ROUND 1 — "SURFACE LEVEL" (1:00–2:15)

**[LOWER THIRD GRAPHIC: ROUND 1 — EASY]**

[MAYA draws the first card. Reads it.]

MAYA: "Okay. 'What's one thing you told your friends about me after we broke up?'"

[Beat. JORDAN rubs the back of his neck. His eyes drift toward the mirror — catches himself — looks back at MAYA.]

JORDAN: "That you were… the most stubborn person I've ever met. And that I kind of respected it."

MAYA: [surprised, softening slightly] "Oh. That's… actually not bad."

[CUT TO MIRROR ROOM. SAM's jaw tightens almost imperceptibly.]

SAM: [to DEV, quiet] "He never told *me* that."

DEV: [sipping water, watching intently, red card still in hand] "Mmhm."

[CAMERA holds on DEV's fingers resting on the red card. They're not playing it yet. But they're thinking about it.]

[BACK TO TABLE. JORDAN draws a card.]

JORDAN: "'Did you ever go through my phone?'"

[MAYA reaches for the tequila without a word. Pours. Drinks. Sets the glass down hard.]

MAYA: "Next."

JORDAN: [leaning back, eyebrows up] "That's a yes."

MAYA: "That's a *drink*. Those are the rules."

[CUT TO MIRROR ROOM. DEV sets their water down slowly.]

DEV: "She went through his phone?"

SAM: [grim smile] "Oh, this is just getting started."

[WIDE SHOT through the one-way glass: JORDAN and MAYA at the table, small and bright under the studio lights, unaware of exactly how closely they're being studied. The red cards sit in SAM's and DEV's laps like loaded weapons.]

[LOWER THIRD GRAPHIC fades in: **ROUND 2 — COMING UP**]

[JORDAN glances at the mirror one more time.]

JORDAN: [under his breath] "Please don't be writing something down back there."

[CUT TO MIRROR ROOM. SAM is, in fact, writing something down on the back of the red card.]
