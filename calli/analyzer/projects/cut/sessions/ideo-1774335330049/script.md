## ACT I — COLD OPEN HOOK (0:00–0:15)

*[WIDE SHOT: Two strangers — JALEN (22, oversized vintage tee, silver rings) and MIKA (21, baby tee, micro bangs) — sit across from each other at a minimalist white table. Bottle of Casamigos and two shot glasses between them. Soft diffused overhead lighting. Camera slowly pushes in from wide two-shot to medium. Neither has spoken yet. The silence is loud.]*

**MIKA:** *[nervous laugh, eyes darting to the tequila]* "So… we're really doing this."

**JALEN:** *[leans back, cracks knuckles]* "I don't even know your name and I'm about to tell you my body count. Cool. Cool cool cool."

*[SMASH CUT TO: CUT Analytics title card — "TRUTH OR DRINK: GEN Z EDITION" — with a punchy bass hit. Quick flash-montage of reactions from later in the video: someone choking on tequila, someone covering their face, someone screaming "NOOO." Back to the table.]*

---

## ACT II — THE SETUP: "SO… THIS IS GONNA GET MESSY"

*[MEDIUM TWO-SHOT. Both seated. The energy is first-day-of-school awkward. A PRODUCER (off-camera) speaks.]*

**PRODUCER (O.C.):** "Alright. Introduce yourselves. You've never met before today."

**MIKA:** *[small wave]* "Hi. I'm Mika. She/her. I'm a Scorpio, which already tells you everything."

**JALEN:** *[laughs]* "I'm Jalen. He/him. Gemini sun, Pisces moon — so I will cry at some point today."

**MIKA:** "Oh, we're BOTH gonna cry."

**PRODUCER (O.C.):** "Here's how it works. You take turns pulling a question card. You either answer honestly — or you take the shot. No in-between."

**JALEN:** *[picks up the tequila, inspects the label]* "At least they gave us the good stuff."

**MIKA:** *[deadpan to camera]* "They're trying to get us drunk so we overshare. And honestly? [[H01:test whether self-aware humor in setup increases watch-through rate|It's gonna work.]]"

*[Both laugh. The tension cracks just enough. Camera settles into alternating over-the-shoulder singles.]*

---

## ROUND 1 — "THE WARM-UP" (Light & Flirty)

*[MIKA draws the first card from the deck. She reads it aloud.]*

**MIKA:** *[reading]* "What's the most embarrassing thing on your screen time report?"

**JALEN:** *[immediately]* "Truth. Seven hours of TikTok. In one day. On a Tuesday."

**MIKA:** "A TUESDAY?!"

**JALEN:** "I had COVID! I was in bed! But also… three of those hours were TikTok Shop."

**MIKA:** *[dying]* "Did you BUY anything?"

**JALEN:** "I bought a portable blender I've used exactly once."

*[Both laughing. Camera catches MIKA wiping her eye.]*

---

*[JALEN draws the next card.]*

**JALEN:** *[reading, then wincing]* "Have you ever posted a thirst trap and then deleted it because it didn't get enough likes?"

**MIKA:** *[long pause. Stares at the shot glass. Stares at Jalen. Stares at the camera.]*

**MIKA:** "…How many likes counts as 'not enough'?"

**JALEN:** "Oh, you're COOKED."

**MIKA:** *[reaches for the shot glass, then pulls back]* "Okay FINE. Yes. It was a mirror selfie. It got 43 likes in an hour and I panicked."

**JALEN:** "Forty-three is NOT bad!"

**MIKA:** "It is when your ex got 200 on a SUNSET PHOTO. A sunset, Jalen. The SUN did the work."

*[JALEN howls. Camera pushes in on MIKA's mock-indignant face.]*

---

### CONFESSIONAL BEAT #1

*[CUT TO: Solo interview setup. JALEN against a plain backdrop, slightly tighter framing.]*

**JALEN:** *[to camera]* "She's a Scorpio and she deleted a thirst trap over 43 likes? That's the most Scorpio thing I've ever heard. I'm already scared of the next round."

---

## ROUND 2 — "GETTING PERSONAL" (The Temperature Rises)

*[Back at the table. The bottle is slightly less full. MIKA draws.]*

**MIKA:** *[reading]* "What's one thing you pretend to care about but secretly don't?"

**JALEN:** *[exhales slowly]* "…Astrology."

**MIKA:** *[jaw drops]* "You literally just told me your BIG THREE."

**JALEN:** "I know my big three because EVERYONE asks! I don't actually believe Mercury is messing up my emails!"

**MIKA:** *[hand over heart]* "Mercury retrograde is REAL and she has PERSONALLY ruined my relationships."

**JALEN:** "No, babe, that was you."

*[MIKA gasps. Beat. Then she laughs so hard she almost knocks over the tequila.]*

---

*[JALEN draws.]*

**JALEN:** *[reading]* "Have you ever stayed in a situationship way too long because you were afraid of being alone?"

*[The energy shifts. Both go quiet for a beat. The camera holds on MIKA's face.]*

**MIKA:** *[softly]* "Yeah. I have."

*[Beat.]*

**MIKA:** "I was in this thing for like eight months. He never called me his girlfriend. I kept telling my friends 'we don't need labels' but I was literally journaling about it every night like… 'why won't he choose me?'"

**JALEN:** *[genuine]* "That's real. I'm sorry."

**MIKA:** "It's okay. I chose myself eventually. But it took way too long."

*[Camera lingers. Neither reaches for the tequila. This is the moment that makes the video shareable — [[H02:test whether vulnerable mid-video moment improves comment engagement vs. keeping it comedic throughout|raw honesty between strangers.]]]*

**JALEN:** *[after a beat]* "For what it's worth? You seem like somebody who knows her worth now."

**MIKA:** *[smiles, eyes glassy]* "Jalen, if you make me cry on camera I WILL fight you."

*[They both laugh. Tension releases. Camera pulls back to the two-shot.]*

---

### CONFESSIONAL BEAT #2

*[CUT TO: Solo interview. MIKA alone.]*

**MIKA:** *[to camera, wiping under her eye]* "I didn't expect to get that honest with a stranger. But like… he made it feel safe? I don't know. Gen Z doesn't talk about this stuff enough. We just post 'I'm healing' with a beach photo and call it therapy."

---

## ROUND 3 — "THE SPICY ROUND" (No Mercy)

*[Back at the table. Energy has shifted — they're bonded now, which makes the spicy questions hit different. MIKA draws.]*

**MIKA:** *[reads, then immediately covers her mouth]* "Oh NO."

**JALEN:** "Read it. READ IT."

**MIKA:** *[reading]* "What's your body count, and do you think it's too high, too low, or just right?"

**JALEN:** *[stares at the shot glass for a long time]*

**MIKA:** "Tick tock, Gemini."

**JALEN:** *[picks up the shot, holds it]* "I'm gonna answer AND drink because I need the courage."

*[He throws back the shot. Winces.]*

**JALEN:** "Eleven."

**MIKA:** "That's so… normal?"

**JALEN:** "I KNOW, but my mom watches these videos!"

**MIKA:** *[to camera]* "Hi, Jalen's mom. He's doing great."

**JALEN:** *[head in hands]* "I'm going to move."

---

*[JALEN draws. Reads silently. His face goes blank.]*

**JALEN:** *[reading]* "Have you ever sent a risky text to the wrong person?"

**MIKA:** *[reaches for the tequila IMMEDIATELY]*

**JALEN:** "WAIT — you're not even gonna —"

**MIKA:** *[pours, shoots, grimaces]* "Some things die with me, Jalen."

**JALEN:** "It was a NUDE, wasn't it."

**MIKA:** *[stone-faced, tequila still burning]* "I said what I said."

**JALEN:** *[to camera]* "It was a nude."

**MIKA:** "NEXT CARD."

---

*[MIKA draws, still recovering.]*

**MIKA:** *[reading]* "What's the most unhinged thing you've done after a breakup?"

**JALEN:** *[no hesitation]* "I made a Spotify playlist called 'Songs That Aren't About You' and it was 47 songs long and they were ALL about her."

**MIKA:** "Forty… seven."

**JALEN:** "It started with Olivia Rodrigo and ended with Radiohead. That's a JOURNEY."

**MIKA:** "That's a mental health crisis set to music."

**JALEN:** "It got 12 followers though."

**MIKA:** *[losing it]* "TWELVE PEOPLE followed your BREAKDOWN?"

**JALEN:** *[proudly]* "Content is content."

---

### CONFESSIONAL BEAT #3

*[CUT TO: Solo interview. JALEN.]*

**JALEN:** *[to camera]* "She grabbed that shot glass like it owed her money. I respect it. Some truths are worth a hangover to protect."

---

## ROUND 4 — "THE FINAL CARD" (The One That Hits)

*[Back at the table. The bottle is noticeably lighter. Both are slightly flushed, more relaxed, leaning in. The wall between strangers is gone.]*

**PRODUCER (O.C.):** "Last card. Whoever's turn it is — this one's for both of you."

*[JALEN draws. Reads it silently. His expression softens.]*

**JALEN:** *[reading, slowly]* "What's something you're genuinely afraid your generation is getting wrong?"

*[Long beat. Neither reaches for the tequila. The camera holds in a patient medium two-shot.]*

**MIKA:** *[thinking]* "I think… we're so afraid of being cringe that we don't let ourselves be sincere. Like, everything has to be ironic or detached. And I think we're gonna look back and realize we were so busy being cool that we forgot to actually… feel stuff."

*[Beat.]*

**JALEN:** "Yeah. I think we perform our lives instead of living them. Like I'll be at a concert and my first thought isn't 'this is beautiful,' it's 'I need to get this on my story.' And that scares me."

**MIKA:** "It scares me too."

*[They look at each other. Genuine. The camera doesn't cut away.]*

**JALEN:** *[raising his shot glass]* "To feeling stuff."

**MIKA:** *[raising hers]* "To being cringe."

*[They clink. They drink. They wince. They laugh.]*

**MIKA:** "That was terrible tequila, by the way."

**JALEN:** "It was Casamigos!"

**MIKA:** "I said what I said!"

---

## ACT III — THE CLOSING

*[WIDE SHOT pulling back to the original framing. Both are laughing, loose, connected. The table between them feels smaller than it did at the start.]*

**PRODUCER (O.C.):** "Last question — off the cards. Would you hang out again?"

**JALEN:** "Absolutely. Mika, you're terrifying and I love it."

**MIKA:** "Jalen, you're a Gemini who made a 47-song breakup playlist. We're literally the same person."

*[They stand. Hug across the table. It's slightly awkward because of the table. That makes it better.]*

**JALEN:** "Also I'm definitely gonna follow her thirst traps now."
