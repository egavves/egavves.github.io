**Underdeveloped Contrast Pairing**
Jalen and Mika have visual identity (vintage tee, micro bangs) but zero established dynamic tension before the first question lands.

### 5. Early Retention ⚠️
**Cold Open Compression Needed**

### 6. Stakes ✅
**Asymmetric Emotional Jeopardy**
One person submitted the screenshots anonymously — the other doesn't know which lies were flagged, creating genuine, unscripted dread.

### 7. Escalation ⚠️
**Escalation Architecture Unbuilt**
Storyboard shows four scenes but no documented drink-tier progression or emotional peak spacing — peak_spacing_cv is LASSO-stable for retention.

### 8. Fun & Authenticity ✅

### 9. Cut Formula ✅
**Forensic Twist on Established Format**
CUT's signature white seamless set plus the screenshot mechanic is a clean formula extension — familiar container, genuinely new combustible content.

### 10. Brand Values ✅
**Radical Honesty Under Pressure**
Submitted-by-your-ex receipts enforce CUT's core brand promise: no performance, no escape, only the truth you already put in writing.
