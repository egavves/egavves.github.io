## PRE-PRODUCTION NOTES

**Cast:** 4 players — MAYA (22, chaotic energy, oversharer), DEV (21, dry humor, tries to seem unbothered), PRIYA (23, therapist-brained, secretly messy), JORDAN (20, youngest, wildcard)

**Set:** Low table, floor cushions, ring lighting, four shot glasses + a bottle of something pink and sparkling. Stickers and sticky notes on the question card box.

**Tone:** Warm, chaotic, real. NOT performative. The laughs should feel earned.

## INTRO SEQUENCE — 0:15–1:00

*[Wide shot. All four players settle in. JORDAN is already nervous, adjusting their position. MAYA is shuffling the card deck with suspicious enthusiasm.]*

**MAYA:** "Okay, for anyone who doesn't know how this works — you get a card, you answer the truth, OR you drink. Simple."

**PRIYA:** "There's nothing simple about this game."

**DEV:** "Last time I played this I didn't speak to my cousin for three weeks."

**JORDAN:** *(quietly)* "Wait, is that a warning or a flex?"

**DEV:** "...Yes."

*[Laughter. MAYA sets the deck down ceremonially.]*

**MAYA:** "We're going in rounds. Each round gets... spicier. [[H01:test whether escalation framing increases viewer retention past 3-min mark|Everyone agreed to escalate]]?"

**PRIYA:** "Agreed."

**DEV:** "Fine."

**JORDAN:** "I'm going to regret this."

**MAYA:** "Probably. Okay — ROUND ONE. Warm-up cards. Nobody's getting hurt. Yet."

## ROUND ONE: "ICEBREAKER" — 1:00–2:15
### *[Emotional Floor: Warmth/Nerves | Ceiling: Mild Roast]*

*[MAYA draws first. Reads card aloud.]*

---

**CARD 1 — MAYA draws:**

**MAYA:** *(reading)* "What's something you've lied about on your Instagram?"

*[Beat. MAYA doesn't even blink.]*

**MAYA:** "My skincare routine. I post like I use twelve products. I use water and prayer."

*[JORDAN loses it. DEV nods slowly like this confirms something.]*

**DEV:** "I KNEW the glass skin was fake."

**MAYA:** "It's called good lighting, Devon."

---

**CARD 2 — DEV draws:**

**DEV:** *(reading)* "What's a trend you secretly hate but pretend to like?"

*[Long pause. DEV picks up their drink, looks at it, sets it back down.]*

**DEV:** "Okay. Journaling. I don't journal. I've bought seven journals. They're all empty except for one entry that says 'Day 1: I'm going to journal now.' That's it. That's all seven journals."

**PRIYA:** *(gasping)* "The SAME entry?"

**DEV:** "The same entry."

*[CONFESSIONAL CUT — DEV alone, talking to camera:]*
**DEV:** "The journals are a cry for help. I know that. Moving on."

---

**CARD 3 — PRIYA draws:**

**PRIYA:** *(reading)* "Have you ever soft-launched someone you weren't actually dating?"

*[PRIYA immediately reaches for her drink.]*

**MAYA:** "WAIT — DRINK MEANS YOU DID IT."

**PRIYA:** *(already drinking)* "I know what it means."

*[SCREAMING. JORDAN is kicking their legs. DEV is pointing.]*

**DEV:** "WHO WAS IT."

**PRIYA:** *(wiping mouth)* "The drink is the answer."

---

**CARD 4 — JORDAN draws:**

**JORDAN:** *(reading slowly)* "What's the most embarrassing thing you've done for someone's attention?"

*[JORDAN turns red immediately.]*

**JORDAN:** "I... started liking someone's posts from 2019. On purpose. So they'd see my name in their notifications."

**MAYA:** "Jordan."

**JORDAN:** "It worked though."

**DEV:** "Did it?"

**JORDAN:** "...They blocked me."

*[The table dissolves. MAYA is crying laughing. PRIYA reaches over and pats JORDAN's hand.]*

**PRIYA:** "That's growth, babe. That's a story."

---

*[TRANSITION: MAYA fans out the next set of cards — different color, slightly larger.]*

**MAYA:** "Okay. Round one survived. Round two is the 'We're Getting Real' round. These cards are orange. Orange means spicy."

**JORDAN:** "I don't like orange."

---

## ROUND TWO: "LIGHT SHADE" — 2:15–3:45
### *[Emotional Floor: Laughter/Exposure | Ceiling: Hook/Cliffhanger]*

*[CONFESSIONAL CUT — PRIYA to camera, before Round 2 starts:]*
**PRIYA:** "Round two is where people start actually sweating. You can see it. The vibe shifts. Someone's going to say something they can't take back."

---

**CARD 5 — PRIYA draws:**

**PRIYA:** *(reading)* "Have you ever talked badly about someone in this room — to someone outside this room?"

*[Silence. Loaded silence. PRIYA looks at the drink. Looks at the group.]*

**PRIYA:** "I'm going to answer this truthfully, which means I need everyone to remember we're friends first."

**DEV:** "That's a yes."

**PRIYA:** "It's a nuanced yes. I told my roommate that Dev gives terrible advice about relationships—"

**DEV:** "THAT'S FAIR ACTUALLY."

**PRIYA:** "—and that Maya once texted me forty-seven times in one night—"

**MAYA:** "You were going through something!"

**PRIYA:** "YOU were going through something. I was just awake."

*[Laughter, but MAYA is slightly squinting at PRIYA. Noted.]*

---

**CARD 6 — DEV draws:**

**DEV:** *(reading)* "What's something your parents think about you that's completely wrong?"

*[DEV exhales. This one lands differently.]*

**DEV:** "They think I have a plan."

*[Beat.]*

**DEV:** "Like, a real plan. They think I know what I'm doing. And I just... I keep buying time. Every time they ask, I give them a different version of 'I'm figuring it out' and they believe me because they want to."

*[Quiet. This one hits. JORDAN nods slowly.]*

**PRIYA:** "Dev, that's—"

**DEV:** "Don't therapist-voice me right now, I'm fine."

*[Small laugh. But it's softer now.]*

---

**CARD 7 — JORDAN draws:**

**JORDAN:** *(reading)* "Have you ever ghosted someone and then watched their stories anyway?"

**JORDAN:** "Oh, this is literally my personality."

*[Everyone points at JORDAN simultaneously.]*

**JORDAN:** "What! It's called staying informed! You ghost someone, that doesn't mean you stop being curious about their life!"

**MAYA:** "That's literally the definition of haunting someone."

**JORDAN:** "I prefer 'passive observer.'"

**DEV:** "You're a ghost with a ring light."

*[JORDAN drinks anyway, out of solidarity.]*

---

**CARD 8 — MAYA draws:**

**MAYA:** *(reading, then pausing)*

*[MAYA reads the card. Her face does something complicated. She looks up.]*

**MAYA:** "Okay this one's... okay. 'What's something you've never told anyone in this room?'"

*[The energy shifts. This is the [[H02:test whether a single moment of genuine vulnerability increases share rate vs. laugh-heavy segments|pivot moment]].]*

*[MAYA holds the card. Looks around.]*

**MAYA:** "I almost didn't come today. Like, I almost texted and said I was sick. Because lately I've been feeling kind of... invisible? Like I'm funny and I'm loud and everyone thinks I'm fine, and I just—"

*[She stops. Waves her hand.]*

**MAYA:** "Anyway. That's the truth."

*[Silence. Then PRIYA reaches over. Then DEV. Then JORDAN. All three hands land on MAYA at once.]*

**JORDAN:** "We see you."

*[MAYA laughs, but her eyes are wet.]*

**MAYA:** "Okay don't make it a whole thing—"

**DEV:** "Too late. It's a whole thing."

---

*[TRANSITION — PRIYA picks up the RED cards.]*

**PRIYA:** "Red round. These are the ones the producers told us might cause 'interpersonal friction.'"

**DEV:** "They said that?"

**PRIYA:** "They said 'interpersonal friction.' I'm choosing to believe that means feelings, not a lawsuit."

## ROUND THREE: "REAL CONFESSION" — 3:45–5:30
### *[Emotional Floor: Vulnerability/Tension | Ceiling: Tears or Anger]*

*[CONFESSIONAL CUT — JORDAN to camera:]*
**JORDAN:** "By Round Three, the drinks are hitting and the walls are down. Which is either beautiful or a disaster. Usually both."

---

**CARD 9 — DEV draws:**

**DEV:** *(reading)* "When did you last cry, and what was it actually about?"

*[DEV stares at the card for a beat too long.]*

**DEV:** "Two weeks ago. In my car. In a parking garage."

**MAYA:** "What happened?"

**DEV:** "Nothing happened. That's the thing. Nothing happened, and I just... sat there and cried because I was tired. Not like, sleep-tired. Just... tired."

*[PRIYA nods. MAYA nods. JORDAN nods. They all know this tired.]*

**DEV:** "And then I drove home and made pasta and watched a YouTube video about deep sea fish and felt better."

**PRIYA:** "The deep sea fish pipeline is real."

**DEV:** "Extremely real."

---

**CARD 10 — JORDAN draws:**

**JORDAN:** *(reading)* "Is there someone in your life you need to have a conversation with but keep avoiding? Why?"

*[JORDAN laughs nervously. Picks up the drink. Puts it down.]*

**JORDAN:** "My dad. We haven't really talked since I came out to him. Like, he wasn't mean about it. He just got very... quiet. And I don't know what to do with quiet. I can handle angry, I can handle happy, I cannot handle quiet."

*[Nobody laughs. This is real.]*

**MAYA:** *(softly)* "Have you tried reaching out?"

**JORDAN:** "I write the text like every other week. I just don't send it."

**PRIYA:** "What does the text say?"

**JORDAN:** "It just says, 'Hey, can we talk?' That's it. Five words. I can't send five words."

*[PRIYA squeezes JORDAN's arm.]*

**PRIYA:** "The five words are the bravest part. You'll send them."

---

**CARD 11 — PRIYA draws:**

**PRIYA:** *(reading)* "Have you ever stayed in a relationship — romantic or friendship — way longer than you should have, because you were scared of being alone?"

*[PRIYA laughs, short and sharp.]*

**PRIYA:** "I mean. I'm a psychology major. I know exactly what I do. I know the clinical name for it, I know the root cause, I know the coping strategies. And I still do it. Every time."

**DEV:** "The cobbler's kids have no shoes."

**PRIYA:** "I am BAREFOOT, Dev. I am walking on hot asphalt with no shoes, full clinical knowledge, just choosing pain."

*[Laughter, but it's the kind that comes from recognition.]*

---

**CARD 12 — MAYA draws:**

**MAYA:** *(reading)* "What's something you're genuinely scared of about your future?"

*[MAYA doesn't reach for the drink this time.]*

**MAYA:** "That I'll be 35 and I'll have been performing my whole life. Like — being the funny one, being the hype person, being the one who holds everyone together — and I'll wake up one day and realize I never actually let anyone take care of me. Because I never let them."

*[Quiet. Real quiet.]*

**MAYA:** "That one got away from me."

**JORDAN:** "No, that was... that was real."

**DEV:** "For the record — we'd take care of you."

**PRIYA:** "We are actively trying to take care of you right now."

*[MAYA covers her face with the card. We can see her smile behind it.]*

---

*[TRANSITION — DEV picks up the GOLD cards. They're smaller. There are only four.]*

**DEV:** "Gold round. One card each. These are the ones you answer for the person to your LEFT."

**JORDAN:** "Wait, so someone else reads MY card?"

**DEV:** "Someone else reads your card."

**JORDAN:** "That's so much worse."

---

## ROUND FOUR: "CATHARSIS/CONNECTION" — 5:30–7:00
### *[Emotional Floor: Earned Emotion | Ceiling: Group Hug or Walk-Off]*

*[CONFESSIONAL CUT — MAYA, before Round 4:]*
**MAYA:** "Gold round is different. It's not about exposing yourself anymore. It's about what you see in each other. Which is somehow scarier."

---

**GOLD CARD — DEV reads for MAYA (to their left):**

**DEV:** *(reading)* "What do you think this person is most afraid people will find out about them?"

*[DEV looks at MAYA. MAYA looks at DEV. A long beat.]*

**DEV:** "I think... you're afraid people will find out you need things. That you have needs and wants and bad days and you're not always okay. Because your whole identity is being okay. Being the one who makes it okay for everyone else."

*[MAYA is very still.]*

**DEV:** "And I think you know that, and I think you're working on it, and I think you're doing better than you think you are."

*[MAYA blinks. Looks away. Looks back.]*

**MAYA:** "You can't just say things like that."

**DEV:** "Too bad. It's the gold round."

---

**GOLD CARD — MAYA reads for PRIYA:**

**MAYA:** *(reading)* "What do you think this person is most proud of that they'd never say out loud?"

*[MAYA looks at PRIYA warmly.]*

**MAYA:** "I think you're proud of how far you've come. Like — you moved to a new city, you don't know that many people, you built this whole life basically from scratch, and you make it look easy. And I think you're proud of that but you'd never say it because you're too busy analyzing everyone else."

*[PRIYA laughs, but her eyes are shiny.]*

**PRIYA:** "I hate how accurate that is."

**MAYA:** "I know you."

---

**PRIYA:** *(reading)* "What do you think this person needs to hear right now that no one has said to them?"

*[
