## DEMOGRAPHICS & ENERGY

**Age Range:** 19–26 (sweet spot: 21–23)
**Core Energy Profile:** Unpolished authenticity over performance. Gen Z doesn't trust "TV ready." They trust *real*.

**Personality Types (MBTI as shorthand, not gospel):**
- High ENFPs and INFJs — emotionally fluent, quick to pivot
- At least one INTJ for dry-anchor contrast
- Avoid pure ESFJs — they tend to *manage* the room rather than *crack open* in it

**Diversity Requirements (non-negotiable):**
- Racial/ethnic representation across all 4 cast seats
- At least one openly queer-identified player
- Regional accent diversity (not everyone sounds like LA or NYC)
- Class diversity in *storytelling texture* — not just aesthetics

## WHAT WE VALUE (In Order)

**1. Comfort on Camera Under Pressure**
Can they sit in an awkward silence without filling it with noise? Do they *lean in* when a question lands hard, or do they deflect immediately? We want leaners.

**2. Experience with Vulnerability Formats**
Have they done confessional content? Storytelling open mics? Therapy TikTok? Reddit AMAs read aloud? [[H02:test whether candidates with confessional social content history outperform trained actors in authenticity scoring by viewer panels|Prior vulnerability format experience likely predicts higher audience trust scores than acting credits]] — we believe this strongly.

**3. Social Native Fluency**
They understand *why* a moment is clippable. Not because they're chasing clips — because they live in the language. There's a difference between someone who *performs* for the algorithm and someone who *thinks* in it.

**4. Group Chemistry Instinct**
Do they read the room and *respond* to it, or do they wait for their turn to talk? We need players who treat this as a conversation, not a panel.

## RED FLAGS TO AVOID

| 🚩 Red Flag | Why We Skip It |
|-------------|----------------|
| **Follower count** | Vanity metric. 2.3M followers who came for dancing ≠ vulnerability on camera |
| **Traditional acting credits** | Training teaches *control*. This format rewards *loss of control* |
| **Brand deal history** | Brand-trained talent self-edits. We need people who haven't learned to protect themselves yet |
| **"I'm an open book" self-description** | Usually means they've rehearsed their trauma, not lived in it |
| **Over-prepared audition answers** | If they nail the callback too cleanly, cut them |

## GENDER MIX & REGIONAL VOICE

**Gender Mix:**
- Minimum 2 non-male-identifying players
- At least 1 player who exists outside the binary — not as a token story beat, but as a *casting baseline*
- Chemistry reads must include cross-gender pairings that don't default to flirtation as the only tension type

**Regional Voice:**
- **Avoid:** Full LA cast, full NYC cast
- **Target voices:** Midwest-raised, Southern-coded, first-gen American accent texture, AAPI regional inflection
- Accent ≠ character. We want regional *worldview*, not regional *performance*
